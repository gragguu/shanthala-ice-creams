The Legacy of Shantala Ice Creams

Shantala Ice Creams began with a simple dream – a grandpa and grandma who had a deep love for ice cream and a small refrigerator. From these humble beginnings, they created a place that would become a local favourite in Rajajinagar, Bangalore.

With a commitment to quality, love, and unique homemade flavours, they served their community with passion and joy. Today, their legacy lives on as their son and grandson carry forward the tradition, introducing new and exciting flavours while holding onto the original values that started it all.

From colourful cones to classic sundaes, every scoop at Shantala Ice Creams tells a story – a story of family, flavour, and the joy of simple moments.
